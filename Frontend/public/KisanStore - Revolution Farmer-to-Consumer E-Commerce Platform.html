FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Have Questions? We Have Answers

Find answers to common questions about our platform, features, and how to get started with direct farm-to-consumer trading.

Our 3D marketplace uses WebGL, Three.js, and Babylon.js to create an immersive shopping experience. Farmers can create 3D models of their farms and products, which buyers can explore, rotate, and inspect from all angles. You can access the 3D marketplace from both desktop and mobile devices. For the best experience, we recommend using a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.

Try Demo

We use Ethereum smart contracts and Hyperledger Fabric to create a secure escrow system. When you place an order, your payment is held in a smart contract until you confirm receipt of the products. This protects both buyers and farmers from fraud. All transactions are recorded on the blockchain, making them transparent and tamper-proof. We support both cryptocurrency (Ethereum, Bitcoin) and traditional payment methods (UPI, credit cards, Paytm, Razorpay).

Learn More About Blockchain Security

Our AI pricing system analyzes multiple data points to suggest optimal prices for farmers. It continuously scrapes data from mandis across India using web scraping technologies. The system considers historical price trends, current market demand, seasonal variations, quality grades, and even weather conditions. We use LSTM and Prophet models for time series forecasting to predict future price movements. This helps farmers maximize their profits while keeping prices fair for consumers.

Our AI crop disease detection system has an accuracy rate of over 90% for most common crop diseases. It uses advanced computer vision models (YOLOv8 and EfficientNet) trained on millions of images of healthy and diseased crops. Farmers can upload photos of their crops, and within seconds, the AI will identify any diseases and recommend appropriate treatments. The system continuously improves as more farmers use it. For edge cases, our AI connects farmers with agricultural experts for personalized advice.

Setting up a video call is simple. When browsing farm products, you'll see a "Video Call" button on the farmer's profile. Click this button, select a convenient time slot, and send a request. Once the farmer accepts, you'll receive a confirmation notification. At the scheduled time, you'll get an alert to join the call. Our video calls use WebRTC for secure, peer-to-peer connections without intermediaries. You can discuss growing practices, see the farm, and negotiate directly with the farmer.

Our smart logistics system uses IoT sensors and GPS modules to track your orders in real-time. After placing an order, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. From the app or website, you can see the exact location of your delivery, estimated arrival time, and even the temperature of perishable items. Our AI-based route optimization algorithm ensures the fastest and most efficient delivery route. For sensitive products like dairy or certain vegetables, you'll also get temperature alerts to ensure freshness.

Our platform is fully multilingual and currently supports 12 Indian languages: Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Punjabi, Odia, and Assamese. Both the website interface and the AI chatbot can communicate in all these languages. This ensures that farmers and consumers from all regions of India can use the platform comfortably. We're constantly working to add more languages and improve our translations.

Registering as a farmer is simple. Click on the "Register as Farmer" button at the top of the website or download our mobile app. Fill in your details including name, location, crops you grow, and farm size. Upload verification documents like your Kisan Credit Card, Aadhaar, or land records. Our team will verify your details within 24-48 hours. Once verified, you can create your farm profile, upload product images (or 3D models), set prices (with AI assistance), and start selling directly to consumers across India.
